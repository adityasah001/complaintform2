About SIMMI FOUNDATION



Simmi Foundation envisions to develop a society based on legitimate rights,
equity, justice, honesty, social sensitivity and a culture of service in which all are self-reliant.
We look forward to alleviate poverty and enhance the dignty of poor in the world by addressing basic, but neglected issues of poor.
Education is one of the means through which we can acheive our goals.Women Empowerment is another mode of development, as women are an important section of our society and their empowerment can bring socio-economic and political
progress of the country.
